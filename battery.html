“Empowering Fisherfolk, Protecting Oceans.”

The Issue: Single-Use Batteries & Its Impact

Compressor divers in Bantayan use disposable batteries for night fishing.

Each diver discards up to 66 batteries per month directly into the ocean.

Across Bantayan, this amounts to 554,400 batteries per year, costing PHP 16.6M annually.

This creates an environmental and financial burden on fisherfolk and marine ecosystems.

The Solution: Rechargeable Flashlight Systems

GOODLand launched the BATERI Project in December 2021.

Distributed 270 rechargeable flashlight systems to compressor divers.

This reduces pollution and saves fishers money while strengthening environmental awareness and community collaboration.
